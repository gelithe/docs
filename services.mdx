## Strategy & Roadmapping

<a id="strategy" />

We help organisations move beyond good intentions to credible, measurable sustainability strategies that drive real business value.

**What's included:**

- Materiality assessment and ESG gap analysis
- Science-based target setting (SBTi-aligned)
- Net-zero pathway design
- ESG reporting frameworks — GRI, CSRD, TCFD, ISSB
- Carbon footprint baselining (Scope 1, 2, and 3)
- Stakeholder engagement and narrative development

<Note>
  Every roadmap is built around your business model, sector, and growth stage — not copied from a generic framework.
</Note>
