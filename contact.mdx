<Steps>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do you work with organisations outside the UK/EU?">
    Yes — we work with clients globally. Most advisory and training work is delivered remotely, with in-person options available for intensive engagements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does a typical engagement last?">
    Strategy engagements typically run 6–12 weeks. Advisory retainers are monthly. Workshops are one-off or in a series. We're flexible — let's talk about what suits your needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="We're just getting started with sustainability — is that OK?">
    Absolutely. Many of our clients come to us at the very beginning. That's often the best time — we help you build strong foundations rather than retrofit them later.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you help with sustainability reporting and communications?">
    Yes. We support both the strategic thinking and the external communication — including report writing, messaging, and stakeholder engagement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can you work as a fractional sustainability lead?">
    Yes — this is one of our most popular formats for growing companies that need sustained senior expertise without a full-time hire. Get in touch to discuss.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
